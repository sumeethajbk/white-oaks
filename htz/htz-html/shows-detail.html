Stephan Rogan

Stephan Rogan began his adventure with CKTB in 2008 as a substitute host, quickly becoming enamored with the station's atmosphere, the allure of the Niagara Region, and the incredible support from its dedicated listeners.
A proud alumnus of the Specs Howard School of Broadcast Arts in Detroit, Michigan, Stephan has made his mark on the airwaves from Windsor to London to Ottawa, radiating his vibrant energy and passion for broadcasting.
As the host of "Niagara in the Morning," Stephan is dedicated to making your mornings fantastic. He delivers the latest local, national, and international news, showcases community voices, and adds a sprinkle of humor to keep the vibe upbeat.
When he’s not behind the mic at the White House of Talk, you can find Stephan at the gym, hiking, binge-watching reality shows, or pampering his delightful nieces and nephews.
